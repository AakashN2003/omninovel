# Creator Imagination Engine

## Purpose

Before building a universe, OmniNovel captures the creator's imagination.

Many creators have ideas but struggle with structured worldbuilding questions.

The Creator Imagination Engine converts imagination into structured universe data.

---

## Flow

Creator
↓
Imagination Vault
↓
AI Analysis
↓
Core Questions
↓
Random Questions
↓
Universe DNA

---

## Imagination Vault

The creator can freely describe:

* Dreams
* Scenes
* Characters
* Civilizations
* Power Systems
* Inspirations
* Questions
* Random Ideas

No structure required.

---

## AI Analysis

Extract:

* Concepts
* Themes
* Genres
* Conflicts
* Worldbuilding Elements

---

## Core Questions

Examples:

* Does magic exist?
* Do gods exist?
* Can people become immortal?
* What powers the world?
* What is the civilization level?

---

## Dynamic Questions

Generated from creator ideas.

Example:

Input:
Memories are currency.

Questions:

* Can memories be stolen?
* Can memories be forged?
* Can memories become weapons?
* Can people lose their identity?

---

## Random Question Engine

Purpose:

Discover hidden worldbuilding ideas.

Examples:

* What does the world fear most?
* What is forbidden knowledge?
* What is the biggest lie everyone believes?
* What would make a god cry?
* What happens if the moon disappears?

---

## Universe DNA Generation

Inputs:

* Creator Imagination
* Core Questions
* Dynamic Questions
* Random Questions

Outputs:

* World Laws
* Power System
* Regions
* Factions
* Timeline
* Universe Summary

---

## Goal

The creator should feel:

"I am teaching the AI how my universe works."

instead of:

"I am filling out a form."
